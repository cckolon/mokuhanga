Mokuhanga sisters
 Between 2017-2019, nine artists met by chance at the Mokuhanga Innovation Laboratory in Kawaguchi-ko, Japan, and  catalyzed the formation of the Mokuhanga Sisters print collective.
We are Katie Baldwin, Patty Hudak, Mariko Jesse, Kate MacDonagh, Yoonmi Nam,  Natasha Norman, Mia O, Lucy May Schofield, and Melissa Schulenberg.
