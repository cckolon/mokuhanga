Snake and Moon

Snake & Moon marks 2025, the year of the snake. 
Fearless and transformative, the snake embodies the spirit with which we meet today’s challenges together. Mokuhanga Sisters worked simultaneously over 2 days in January across 6 time zones and 6 countries to create a new suite of prints. The works are printed in the technique of mokuhanga on postcard paper from Echizen and red sumi ink from Nara, Japan.
The 9 original prints are presented in a handmade box in a limited edition of 25. This set is available for a limited time for $350. For inquiries please message us on the contact page of this website. 
All proceeds will go towards supporting future Mokuhanga Sisters projects.
Thank you for your support.
